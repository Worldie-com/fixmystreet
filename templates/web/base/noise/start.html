Please first read the guidance on https://hackney.gov.uk/noise to understand what we can and cannot do about noise, and the types of noise that are considered unwelcome. [% END %]

Legal action is likely to only be taken when the noise is ongoing. This is because the noise has to be deemed a ‘statutory nuisance’ that materially affects the comfort of your home, i.e. loud enough to wake you up at night regularly, or prevent you from concentrating during the day.

One-off incidents are unlikely to be considered for legal action, however, you can still report them to the Council. An enforcement officer will contact you for more details, and may visit the scene and serve an abatement notice to stop the noise.

To report noise, you will need to provide us with:

  • Your address
  • Your phone number
  • An idea of the source of the noise

Your details are confidential unless court action is necessary. Please note that anonymous complaints will not be taken as we need to know who is affected by the problem.
